Shipping Coverage

We currently ship to:

  • All major cities in Kenya (Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, etc.)

  • International shipping available upon request

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days. Custom or made-to-order items may require additional 3-7 days for production. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Packaging

All items are carefully packaged to ensure safe delivery:

  • Items wrapped in protective materials
  • Sturdy shipping boxes
  • Fragile items marked for special handling
  • Eco-friendly packaging when possible

Track Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can track your package status on our website or through the courier's tracking system. For any shipping inquiries, please contact us at info@africanartifacts.com or call +254 712 345678.

Shipping Restrictions

Some items may have shipping restrictions due to their nature. Custom orders and fragile items may require special handling. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the buyer.
