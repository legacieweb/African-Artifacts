About Us

Celebrating the rich heritage of East African craftsmanship

Karibu! Welcome

Welcome to African Artifacts, where every piece tells a story. We believe shopping should be an experience that connects you to the heart of African artistry.

"Utamaduni wetu ni hazina yetu!"
Our culture is our treasure!

Authentic Craftsmanship

Every product reflects the skilled hands of artisans from Kenya and across East Africa. We celebrate the beauty of handwoven textiles, handcrafted carvings, and traditional designs.

"Kitenge si kitenge bila rangi!"
A kitenge isn't a kitenge without its colors!

Preserving Culture

When you shop with us, you're not just buying — you're preserving a legacy. Every purchase supports artisan communities and keeps traditional craftsmanship alive.

"Sanaa, Upendo, Uhalisia!"
Art, Love, Authenticity!

Our Promise

Hakuna matata — we've got something for everyone. Be bold. Be proud. Wear the culture!
