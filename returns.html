✓ Items Eligible for Return

  • Wrong item received
  • Defective or damaged items
  • Items not as described
  • Size doesn't fit (for clothing)
  • Color significantly different from website photos

✗ Items Not Eligible for Return

  • Custom or made-to-order items
  • Intimate apparel for hygiene reasons
  • Items damaged due to misuse
  • Items returned after 30 days
  • Items without original tags or packaging

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, we'll inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund. If approved:

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable
  • Return shipping costs may be covered for defective items
  • Refunds are credited to your original payment method

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or product:

  • Contact us to request an exchange
  • We'll ship the new item once we receive the return
  • Or ship the new item first if available in stock

Shipping Damage Claims

If your item arrives damaged, please:

  • Take photos of the damage immediately
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Keep all original packaging
  • We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue
